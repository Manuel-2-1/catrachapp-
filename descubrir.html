Infinity Bay Spa and Beach Resort

Located on the white sands of Roatán’s iconic West Bay Beach, Infinity Bay Spa & Beach Resort is the perfect destination for families and divers alike. With spacious beachfront villas, a kid-friendly environment, and one of the world’s top dive sites just steps from your door, every moment here is designed to bring you closer to nature—and each other.

Kimpton Grand Roatan Resort And Spa

Silky white sands, gently lapping waves, lush swaying palms—paradise awaits at our Roatán, Bay Islands, Honduras resort. Located on West Bay Beach, Kimpton Grand Roatán Resort & Spa has a prime locale along the world's second-largest barrier reef.

Luna Beach is a unique boutique resort in Roatan nestled between white sand shores and lush jungle on the northern coast of the island.
